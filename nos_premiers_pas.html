Nos premiers pas

A false start, a bright idea, and a different way to wear the South.

Payote presents itself as a young espadrille brand born in Perpignan, between the Pyrenees and the Mediterranean, with the ambition to modernise an ancestral product while keeping French craft and a certain art of living in view. ([payote.fr](https://payote.fr/pages/nos-premiers-pas))

Origins
The setting

Perpignan, between sea light and mountain air.

Their original story places the brand in Perpignan, “between the freshness of the Pyrenees and the scents of the Mediterranean.” It is not framed as Riviera cliché, but as a more specific southern territory — sun, movement, colour, and a daily art of living. ([payote.fr](https://payote.fr/pages/nos-premiers-pas))

That specificity matters. It gives the brand an origin that feels rooted rather than generic, and it explains why Payote can speak both the language of craft and the language of discovery.

From a place, a mistake, and a good idea.
The beginning

An ancestral product, seen with fresh eyes.

On their page, Payote describes itself as the meeting of two worlds, with the goal of reinventing the espadrille by giving it a more modern breath. The emphasis is not only on product, but on inspiration, colour, comfort, and the promise of a quality item made in France. ([payote.fr](https://payote.fr/pages/nos-premiers-pas))

For this redesign, that idea becomes more legible: not just “a young brand”, but a southern French label that modernises a classic without denying where it comes from.

Sometimes a brand starts with a mistake. Sometimes the mistake becomes the signature.

The anecdote

The false start that became the first spark.

Payote’s own version tells the founding anecdote very clearly: Olivier Gelly went to work wearing mismatched espadrilles — one foot in size 41, the other in 43. That same evening, he personalised the pair with paint, colours, and markers so he would not confuse them again. The next day, the customised pair drew strong reactions from clients, and the idea of launching the brand took shape from there. ([payote.fr](https://payote.fr/pages/nos-premiers-pas))

This is the kind of origin story worth keeping: simple, visual, a little funny, and very human. It gives the brand personality before it gives it mythology.
